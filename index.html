Our Policies

We protect every renter and landlord on TEST with clear, fair, and transparent policies.

Terms of Service

Effective: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: February 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering or using TEST ("the Platform"), you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service and all applicable laws. These terms apply to all users including tenants, landlords, and visitors. TEST reserves the right to update these terms at any time.

2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to use TEST. Landlords must have legal ownership or authorized rights to rent the properties they list. All users must provide accurate identity and contact information during registration.

3. Platform Role

TEST is a rental listing marketplace connecting landlords and tenants. We are not a party to any rental agreement and are not responsible for the conduct of landlords, the condition of properties, or the outcome of any tenancy. Users engage with one another at their own discretion.

4. Prohibited Activities

  • Listing properties you do not own or have authority to rent.
  • Posting misleading or fraudulent property information or photographs.
  • Contacting users for purposes unrelated to rental transactions.
  • Collecting deposits or payments outside the TEST platform without disclosure.
  • Using automated scrapers or bots to interact with the platform.

5. Payments & Deposits

Any rental payments, security deposits, or advance payments are made directly between landlords and tenants. TEST does not hold or process rental funds unless using our optional secure payment feature. TEST is not liable for any financial disputes between parties.

6. Termination

TEST reserves the right to suspend or permanently ban accounts that violate these terms. Users may deactivate their accounts at any time by emailing support@test.in.

Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: February 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data: Name, phone number, email address, and government ID (for KYC).
  • Listing Data: Property address, rent amount, photographs, and description.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent between landlords and tenants via the platform.
  • Usage Data: Search queries, pages visited, filters used, and time spent on the platform.
  • Device Data: IP address, device type, browser, and location (with consent).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to provide the rental matching service, verify identities and listings, personalize search results and recommendations, send transactional emails and service notifications, prevent fraud, and comply with Indian laws including the IT Act.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We share only what is necessary with identity verification partners, payment processors, and cloud infrastructure providers ‚Äî all under strict data processing agreements. Your contact details are shared with other users only when you initiate contact.

4. Your Rights

You may access, update, export, or delete your personal data at any time via your account settings or by contacting privacy@test.in. We respond to all data requests within 30 days as required under applicable laws.

Tenant Rights & Protections

Effective: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: February 2025

1. Right to Accurate Information

Every listing on TEST must accurately represent the property. If you find a listing to be materially different from what was described, you can report it and we will investigate within 24 hours. Misrepresented listings are removed and landlords may be banned.

2. Right to Zero Brokerage

TEST is a zero-brokerage platform. No user may charge tenants a brokerage fee for connections made on TEST. Any landlord or intermediary found charging brokerage will be permanently removed from the platform.

3. Security Deposit Protections

  • Landlords may not demand more than 2 months' rent as a security deposit (as per Model Tenancy Act guidelines).
  • Deposits must be refunded within 30 days of vacating the property, minus legitimate deductions.
  • Tenants are advised to use the optional TEST escrow service for deposit protection.

4. Anti-Discrimination Policy

TEST strictly prohibits discrimination against tenants on the basis of religion, caste, gender, marital status, diet, nationality, or disability. Any landlord found engaging in discriminatory practices will be permanently removed. Tenants facing discrimination can report via the in-app reporting tool.

5. Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute with a landlord, contact disputes@test.in with relevant evidence. Our mediation team will attempt to resolve disputes within 5‚Äì7 business days. TEST also connects users with qualified property law professionals for complex cases.

Landlord Guidelines

Effective: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: February 2025

1. Eligibility to List

To list a property on TEST, you must be the legal owner, authorized agent of the owner, or a society/company with rental rights. You must complete identity and property verification before any listing goes live.

2. Listing Quality Standards

  • Minimum 5 clear, current photographs of the property.
  • Accurate BHK configuration, carpet area, and rent amount.
  • Complete amenities checklist ‚Äî furnished, semi-furnished, or unfurnished.
  • Accurate locality and floor information.
  • Availability date and preferred tenant type (if any).

3. Landlord Responsibilities

You must respond to genuine tenant inquiries within 48 hours. You must ensure the property is in a habitable condition before handing over keys. You may not collect any payment from tenants before a signed rental agreement is in place. You are responsible for registering the rental agreement as per applicable state tenancy laws.

4. Prohibited Practices

  • Demanding brokerage, platform fees, or application charges from tenants.
  • Discrimination based on caste, religion, diet, gender, or marital status.
  • Listing properties not in your legal control.
  • Refusing to refund deposits without documented cause.

Cookie Policy

Effective: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: February 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored in your browser when you visit TEST. They help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the platform so we can improve it.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login sessions and core platform functionality.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your search filters, city preference, and display settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized usage data to help us improve features.
  • Marketing Cookies: Optional cookies for personalized property recommendations (opt-in).

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences from your browser settings or TEST's Cookie Preferences panel (accessible from any page footer). Disabling analytics and marketing cookies will not affect your core rental search experience.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Analytics and similar tools which may set their own cookies. These are subject to their respective privacy policies and do not receive personally identifiable information from TEST.
